CO2-emissions	Germany’s progress toward climate targets is mixed. Total greenhouse gas emissions fell by 3.4 % in 2024 to 649 Mt CO₂ equivalent, bringing overall emissions 48.2 % below 1990 levels. Within this total, the transport sector emitted 143.1 million t CO₂ eq in 2024, a slight decline ( 1.4 % versus 2023). Transport’s share of national greenhouse gas emissions has grown to around 22 % (2023) While per kilometre CO₂ emissions of cars have fallen by roughly 13.8 % since 1995, the increase in vehicle kilometres and heavier vehicles has kept overall CO₂ emissions from the road sector almost constant.
